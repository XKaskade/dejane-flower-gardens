About Our Garden

✿

DeJane Flower & Butterfly Gardens was founded with a passion for cultivating beautiful flowers and creating a peaceful environment where visitors can connect with nature.

Our garden features a diverse collection of flowers and butterflies from around the world, meticulously cared for to ensure they thrive in our local Florida climate. From vibrant sunflowers to delicate cherry blossoms, every corner offers a new discovery.
